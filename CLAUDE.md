## Post Writing Guidelines

- Lead with a hook (a bold statement, a counterintuitive insight, or a personal story opener)
- Keep paragraphs short (1–3 lines) for LinkedIn readability
- End with a clear takeaway, question, or call to action
- Use line breaks generously — no walls of text
- Avoid clichés like "game-changer", "excited to announce", "thrilled to share"
- Hashtags at the end: 3–5 relevant ones, not stuffed
